Biography

I am Banda Siva Sankara Vara Prasad, born on August 30, 1968, in Vijayawada, Krishna District, Andhra Pradesh. My academic journey began at AKTP High School, Vijayawada, where I completed my 10th standard. I pursued my intermediate education at Syed Appalsammy College and later earned my degree from Satavahana College, both located in Vijayawada. Driven by a passion for learning, I went on to complete my Master of Arts (MA) from Osmania University(Distance Education). Professionally, I work as a LIC & Health Insurance Agent, assisting clients with their financial and insurance needs and also serve as an part time Accountant. My career reflects a blend of academic diligence, professional integrity, and a commitment to service.
